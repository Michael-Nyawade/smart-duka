# SmartDuka 
*Inventory, Sales & Credit Management System*

## Summary

SmartDuka is a straightforward system geared towards helping SMEs manage stock, sales, and customer accounts efficiently.

The basic concept behind this Smart Shop Manager is moving away from mere transaction recording to accounting for the fact of the money having been paid.

As such, the system allows owners to gain a clearer view of their business status, based on reality rather than assumptions.

---

## The Problem

Small business management often faces multiple difficulties in handling day-to-day operations. While inventory is a factor, other concerns are:

- Providing goods on credit without tracking payments;
- Getting confused by “sales made” and actual money made;
- Missing the distinction between expected profit and real profit;
- Stocking up unnecessary products;
- Running into cashflow issues despite having sales;  

## Proposed Solution

Build a single system that combines:

- Inventory tracking  
- Sales recording  
- Credit management  
- Profit visibility  

All tied together so that business owners can see both **what they’ve earned** and **what they’re still owed**.

---

## Core Features

### 1. Inventory & Sales Tracking
- Track stock as it comes in and goes out  
- Record each sale as a transaction  
- Store cost price and selling price per item  

---

### 2. Cash vs Credit Sales
Every sale is categorized as either:
- **Paid** (cash / mobile money)  
- **Credit** (customer owes money)  

---

### 3. Profit Tracking (Real vs Expected)
Instead of a single profit number:

- **Realized Profit** → profit from fully paid sales  
- **Expected Profit** → includes credit sales  

This helps show the difference between actual earnings and projected earnings.

---

### 4. Credit & Debt Tracking
- Customer profiles  
- Total amount owed per customer  
- Support for partial repayments  
- Full transaction history  

---

### 5. Debt Aging
Track how long debts have been outstanding:

- 0–7 days  
- 8–30 days  
- 30+ days  

Older debts can be flagged as higher risk.

---

### 6. Customer Risk Scoring
Basic rule-based scoring, for example:

- Frequent late payments → high risk  
- Consistent on-time payments → low risk  

---

### 7. Dead Stock Detection
- Identify items not sold within a defined period  
- Suggest reducing stock or discounting  

---

### 8. Low Stock Alerts
- Notify when stock drops below a set threshold  

---

### 9. Daily Insights Dashboard
A simple daily summary showing:

- Sales made today  
- Profit (real vs expected)  
- Credit issued  
- Cash collected vs outstanding  

---
